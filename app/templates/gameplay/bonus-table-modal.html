Instructions

  1. Your total score depends on different subgoals and your aim is to achieve a high total score.
  2. Sometimes when you are working on one of the sub goals, you may gain/lose points for other subgoals.
  3. In this game you earn bonus for improving the score.
  4. You earn bonus only if you beat the initial top score.
  5. Higher the score, higher the bonus!
